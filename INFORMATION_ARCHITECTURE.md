### Detailed Page Structure

#### 1. Home (`/`)
**Purpose:** Welcome visitors and provide clear pathways to key actions
**Content:** Hero section, program overview, impact statistics, latest stories, donation CTA

#### 2. About (`/about`)
**Purpose:** Build trust and credibility with comprehensive organizational information
**Sections:**
- Our Story & History (Founded 2015)
- Mission, Vision & Values
- Leadership Team
- Partnerships (Compassion International, FGCK)
- Community Impact

#### 3. Programs (`/programs`)
**Purpose:** Showcase the three core ministries and their impact
**Main Page:** Overview of all programs
**Detail Pages:**
- **Child Survival Intervention** (`/programs/child-survival`)
- **Child Development through Sponsorship** (`/programs/sponsorship`)  
- **Youth Development** (`/programs/youth-development`)

Each program page includes:
- Program goals and objectives
- Target demographics
- Activities and services
- Success stories
- How to get involved
- Frequently asked questions

#### 4. Get Involved (`/get-involved`)
**Purpose:** Convert visitors into active supporters
**Sections:**
- Volunteer Opportunities
- Prayer Partnership
- Corporate/Church Partnerships
- Internship Programs
- Community Events
- Skills-based Volunteering

#### 5. Donate (`/donate`)
**Purpose:** Facilitate secure, transparent giving
**Features:**
- One-time vs. Monthly giving options
- Suggested donation amounts (KES 1,000 / 2,500 / 5,000 / Custom)
- Program designation options
- Multiple payment methods (M-Pesa, Bank Transfer, Card)
- Transparency about fund usage
- Receipt generation

#### 6. News & Stories (`/stories`)
**Purpose:** Share impact stories and build emotional connection
**Features:**
- Story listings with filters (Program, Year, Impact Type)
- Individual story pages (`/stories/{slug}`)
- Photo galleries within stories
- Social sharing capabilities
- Newsletter signup integration

#### 7. Events (`/events`)
**Purpose:** Promote community engagement and fundraising events
**Features:**
- Upcoming events calendar
- Event detail pages (`/events/{slug}`)
- Registration/RSVP functionality
- Past events archive
- Photo galleries from events

#### 8. Reports & Publications (`/reports`)
**Purpose:** Demonstrate transparency and accountability
**Content:**
- Annual Reports
- Financial Statements
- Impact Assessments
- Program Evaluations
- Research Publications

#### 9. Gallery (`/gallery`)
**Purpose:** Visual storytelling while maintaining dignity
**Features:**
- Program-based photo categories
- Event photo collections
- Consent and dignity statement
- High-quality image display
- Download permissions for media

#### 10. FAQs (`/faq`)
**Purpose:** Address common questions and reduce support burden
**Categories:**
- General Information
- Sponsorship Program
- Volunteering
- Donations & Giving
- Child Protection
- Partnerships

#### 11. Child Protection & Safeguarding (`/safeguarding`)
**Purpose:** Demonstrate commitment to child safety
**Content:**
- Safeguarding policy summary
- Reporting procedures
- Contact information for concerns
- Staff training information
- Volunteer screening process

#### 12. Contact (`/contact`)
**Purpose:** Provide multiple ways to connect
**Features:**
- Contact form
- Office location (generic Lwanda, Kenya)
- Phone and email contacts
- Social media links
- Office hours
- Map integration

#### 13. Privacy Policy (`/privacy`)
**Purpose:** Legal compliance and transparency
**Content:**
- Data collection practices
- Cookie usage
- Third-party integrations
- User rights
- Contact for privacy concerns

#### 14. Terms of Service (`/terms`)
**Purpose:** Legal protection and user guidelines
**Content:**
- Website usage terms
- Donation terms and conditions
- Intellectual property rights
- Limitation of liability

### Why This Structure Works

#### For Sponsors/Donors:
- Clear donation pathway from homepage
- Transparent reporting and impact stories
- Multiple engagement levels (one-time, monthly, sponsorship)
- Trust-building through comprehensive about section

#### For Community Members:
- Easy access to program information
- Local event listings and participation opportunities
- Volunteer pathways clearly outlined
- Contact information readily available

#### For Church Partners:
- Partnership information prominently featured
- Volunteer and group engagement opportunities
- Resource sharing through reports section
- Event collaboration possibilities

#### For Compassion International:
- Brand alignment through consistent messaging
- Proper attribution and partnership recognition
- Safeguarding policies prominently displayed
- Transparent reporting and accountability

### Navigation Hierarchy

**Primary Menu (Desktop/Mobile):**
1. Home
2. About
3. Programs (Dropdown: CSI, CDSP, YDP)
4. Get Involved
5. Donate
6. Stories
7. Contact

**Secondary Menu (Footer):**
- Events
- Gallery  
- Reports
- FAQs
- Safeguarding
- Privacy
- Terms

**Mobile Considerations:**
- Hamburger menu for primary navigation
- Prominent donate button in header
- Quick access to contact information
- Simplified footer with essential links only

This structure prioritizes user goals while maintaining comprehensive information access and building trust through transparency.
